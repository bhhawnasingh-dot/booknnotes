Polynomials Notes - Concept Oriented & Attractive (Hinglish)

1. Polynomial kya hai?

Polynomial ek mathematical expression hai jisme variables aur numbers ka combination hota hai. Example:

P(x) = anxn + an-1xn-1 + ... + a1x + a0
  • x = variable
  • a₀, a₁,..., aₙ = coefficients (numbers)
  • n = degree (highest power of x)

2. Types of Polynomials

  • Monomial: 1 term → 5x³
  • Binomial: 2 terms → x² + 3x
  • Trinomial: 3 terms → x³ + 2x² + 5
  • Higher-degree: 3+ terms → x⁴ - x³ + x - 1

3. Degree of Polynomial

Polynomial ka degree = sabse bada power. Example:

7x⁵ + 3x² - 4 → Degree = 5

4. Standard Form

Polynomial ko descending order me likho (highest power first):

x³ + 2x² - 5x + 1 ✅
2 - 5x + x³ + 2x² ❌

5. Operations on Polynomials

Addition & Subtraction

(3x² + 2x + 1) + (x² - 4x + 5) = 4x² - 2x + 6

Multiplication

(x+2)(x² + 3x + 1) = x³ + 5x² + 7x + 2

Division

Long division ya synthetic division use hota hai.
Remainder theorem: P(x) ÷ (x-a) → remainder = P(a)

6. Factorization

  • Common factor: 6x³ + 9x² = 3x²(2x+3)
  • Grouping: x³ + x² + x + 1 = (x²+1)(x+1)
  • Quadratic: x² + 5x + 6 = (x+2)(x+3)
  • Difference of squares: x² - 9 = (x-3)(x+3)

7. Zeros / Roots

Zeros ya roots = x ki wo values jaha P(x) = 0

x² - 5x + 6 = 0 → (x-2)(x-3) = 0 → zeros: 2, 3

8. Important Theorems

  • Remainder Theorem: P(x) ÷ (x-a) → remainder = P(a)
  • Factor Theorem: Agar P(a)=0 → (x-a) factor hai
  • Fundamental Theorem of Algebra: Degree n ka polynomial exactly n roots rakhta hai (complex numbers bhi)
